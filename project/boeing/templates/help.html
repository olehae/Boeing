How can I help you {{ username }}?



Homepage (but not logged in):

  • If you already have an account, click here to log in or go to Home and click 'Log In' (orange)
  • If you don't have an account, click here to sign up or go to Home and click 'Sign UP' (green)
  • If you only want to get an overview over the available planes and seats, but don't wish to reserve any seats, click here or go to Home and click 'Go to overview anyways' (purple)


Homepage (and logged in):

  • If you want to reserve seats, click here or go to Home and click 'Our current flights' (orange)
  • If you want to cancel reserved seats, go to Home, then click on the boxes of those seats (blue) to select them and click 'Cancel to checked' (purple)


Overview:

  • This page shows available seats: Seat 1A is no longer available in this example (red arrow), but all the others are
  • If you want to reserve a seat now, you may go back to Overview and click on one of the flights (e.g. Flight 01); this will only work, while you are logged in
  • If you aren't logged in and want to change that, go to Log In


Flight/ seat reservation:

  • This is the page for seat reservation
  • To select a seat, click on it (orange)
  • Afterwards, you may either select more seats, or click the 'submit'-button (green) to reserve the selected seat(s)
  • Click here to return to the overview page
